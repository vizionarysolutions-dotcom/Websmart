Usage Guidelines

✓

Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "X" in ARX. This ensures the logo remains prominent and uncluttered.

✓

Minimum Size

Never display the logo smaller than 120px wide for digital applications or 1 inch wide for print materials to maintain legibility.

✓

Color Backgrounds

The logo works best on dark backgrounds (#0A0E27, #1A1F3A) or pure white. Avoid placing on busy patterns or mid-tone backgrounds.

✗

Don't Distort

Never stretch, compress, or rotate the logo. Always maintain the original aspect ratio when scaling.

✗

Don't Modify Colors

Never change the gradient colors or apply effects to the logo. Use only the provided logo files.
